What is FilesToLinks?

This is a service that lets you upload files on the internet and gives you a streamable as well as a direct download of the file.

Learn More

FilesToLinks is currently running on Telegram via bot; Files To Links. Due to Telegram restriction or rather maximum upload size allowed, our bot lets you upload big files or folders of any size up to 4 GB as Telegram allows and the bot as soon as the file upload finish the bot gives you a URL link that you can share as you please.

FilesToLinks bot offer a no-account required file sharing service. You don't need to register and create an account, to upload and share files. You simply start the bot and the bot self-explain everything. When you upload your files to our bot, don't worry about your data's safety. Once your file has been uploaded, it is scanned for viruses as well as if it contains NSFW Contents. Why are files scanned before being uploaded?

Scanning usually takes 3 to 13 seconds depending on file size, we've very powerful servers no matter how large your file is scanning will never take longer than 13s. In association with the highest bandwidth of our servers (950mbps), your files are uploaded in no time, and then the urls of the file are given to you by the bot.
