Privacy Policy



  • We are unable to determine if a file you upload is private to you or someone else, belongs to a company or another organisation, or what it includes because it is already encrypted on your device before you upload it. Images, movies, and certain other sorts of files also have encrypted previews that we create and keep. We collect a little bit of metadata regarding the kind of file, but that does not reveal the information or content that is contained in the file.


  • While they are on our system, all of Your Files are always encrypted. Because any decryption only occurs on your device or that of the person to whom you have given the file links and keys that are established when you grant them access, we never receive, store, or otherwise interact with them in an unencrypted form.


  • When you accept our Terms, you enter into a contract with us for our end-to-end encrypted cloud storage and collaboration services, and as such, we must collect Your Files in order to deliver those services.


  • While you are a subscriber to our services, we retain Your Files, but only in accordance with the suspension and termination rights we have as stated in our Terms. You are required to keep backups of Your Files. We don't promise that there won't be any data loss or that the services won't have bugs.


  • You are responsible for making sure that anybody you grant access to Your Files to abides by the terms of our agreement, our cookie policy, our takedown guidance policy, and this policy. You are in charge of ensuring their adherence.

What if I have a question or concern?

You can always contact us using our Contact Form. Our team would be happy to help and we'll try to get back to you as soon as possible.
