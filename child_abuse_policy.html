Child Abuse Policy



Online file storage is offered by FilesToLinks. FilesToLinks has a zero tolerance policy for anything that depicts the sexual abuse of children or sparks their sexual curiosity. No such content will be permitted on our Service, according to our Terms of use. However, if we become aware that any such content has been stored or sent using our online resources, we immediately take steps to delete it all, disable the user responsible, and notify the appropriate authorities of any associated activity. FilesToLinks hereby retains the right to take additional legal action, including but not limited to working with law enforcement officials and/or governmental organisations to help them bring charges against individuals responsible.

What if I have a question or concern?

You can always contact us using our Contact Form. Our team would be happy to help and we'll try to get back to you as soon as possible.
